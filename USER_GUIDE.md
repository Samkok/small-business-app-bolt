## Business Ownership

### What is an "Owned Business"?

An owned business is one that you created and manage. You have full control over:
- Business settings
- Subscription upgrades
- Team member permissions
- Business deletion

### What About Team Membership?

You can be a **staff member** or **team member** on unlimited businesses created by others. This does **not** count toward your business limit and is completely free.

**Example:**
- You're on the **Free tier** (1 owned business)
- You create "My Coffee Shop" (your 1 owned business)
- A friend adds you as staff to their "Bakery Business"
- You can access both businesses
- Only "My Coffee Shop" counts toward your limit

### Exceeding Business Limits

If you try to create more businesses than your tier allows:
1. A message appears explaining your limit
2. Button to upgrade your plan
3. Button to switch between businesses

**You cannot create additional businesses without upgrading.**

## Sales Transaction Limits

### Free Tier: 50 Sales Limit

On the free tier, you can create up to 50 sales transactions.

**What Counts as a Sale?**
- Each completed sale in your business
- Returns and voids don't count toward the limit
- Sales in different businesses count separately (if you're staff)

**Approaching the Limit:**

When you reach **49 sales** (1 remaining):
- Warning banner appears at top of screen
- "You're almost at your sales limit" message
- Button to upgrade before running out

When you reach **50 sales** (limit reached):
- Red banner appears
- "Upgrade to continue creating sales" message
- Sales creation is blocked
- Can still view all existing data

**What Still Works at the Limit:**
✅ View all sales
✅ View reports and analytics
✅ Manage products
✅ Manage team members
✅ Manage customers
✅ Track expenses

❌ Create new sales

### Upgrading to Remove Limit

All paid tiers (Pro, Pro Plus, Max) include **unlimited sales transactions**.

After upgrading:
- Sales counter resets
- Can immediately create new sales
- No more limits ever

## Read-Only Mode

### What is Read-Only Mode?

When you downgrade from a paid plan with multiple businesses, some businesses become "read-only."

**In read-only mode, you can:**
✅ View all sales data
✅ View reports and analytics
✅ Manage products (add, edit, archive)
✅ Manage team members (add, remove, change roles)
✅ Manage customers (add, edit)
✅ Track and manage expenses
✅ Export data

**In read-only mode, you cannot:**
❌ Create new sales transactions

### Why Does This Happen?

Read-only mode ensures you don't lose data when downgrading, while respecting your new tier's limits.

**Example:**
- You have **Pro Plus** with 3 businesses:
  - Restaurant A
  - Restaurant B
  - Restaurant C
- Your subscription expires
- You're downgraded to **Free** (1 business allowed)
- You choose to keep **Restaurant A** active
- **Restaurant B** and **Restaurant C** become read-only
- All data preserved, but can only create sales in Restaurant A

### Amber Warning Banner

Businesses in read-only mode show an amber/orange banner:

> 📋 **'Restaurant B' is in read-only mode**
> You can view data and manage products, but cannot create sales transactions.
> [Upgrade Plan] [Switch Business]

### Exiting Read-Only Mode

**Option 1: Upgrade Your Plan**
- Upgrade to a tier that supports your number of businesses
- All businesses become active again
- Can create sales in all businesses

**Option 2: Switch Active Businesses**
- Available if you're still at your business limit
- Choose different businesses to be active
- Previously active businesses become read-only

**Option 3: Delete Read-Only Businesses**
- Permanently delete businesses you no longer need
- Frees up slots for new businesses
- **Warning:** Cannot be undone

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Open the app
2. Tap **Settings** → **Subscription**
3. View available plans
4. Tap a plan to see details
5. Tap **Subscribe** button
6. Complete purchase through App Store
7. Changes take effect immediately

### What Happens When You Upgrade?

**Immediately:**
- New limits apply
- All businesses become active (if were read-only)
- Sales creation enabled in all businesses
- Sales transaction limit removed

**No data loss:**
- All existing data preserved
- All sales history retained
- All team members retained

### Who Can Upgrade?

**Only business owners** can upgrade subscriptions.

If you're a **staff member** on someone else's business:
- You cannot upgrade their subscription
- Message shown: "Only the business owner can upgrade"
- Contact the business owner to request upgrade

## Downgrading Your Plan

### How Subscriptions Work

Subscriptions are **prepaid** and last until the end of the billing period.

**Cancellation Process:**
1. Cancel subscription in App Store settings
2. Keep full access until expiration date
3. On expiration, downgrade takes effect

**Example:**
- Subscribed to Pro Plus on Jan 1 (monthly)
- Cancel subscription on Jan 15
- Keep full access until Jan 31
- On Feb 1, downgrade to Free tier

### Choosing Active Businesses

When your subscription expires and you have more businesses than allowed:

**A modal appears:**
- Shows all your owned businesses
- Displays sales count, team size, creation date for each
- Must select which business(es) to keep active
- Cannot be dismissed until selection made

**Selection Rules:**
- **Free tier:** Select exactly 1 business
- **Pro tier:** Select exactly 1 business
- **Pro Plus tier:** Select exactly 3 businesses

After confirming:
- Selected businesses remain **active**
- Non-selected businesses become **read-only**
- Modal dismisses
- Can continue using app

### Avoiding Downgrade Issues

**Before canceling:**
1. Review which businesses you use most
2. Consider consolidating data into one business
3. Delete businesses you no longer need
4. Export data from businesses you might not keep
5. Inform team members of changes

## FAQs

### Can I have multiple subscriptions?

No, you only need one subscription per Apple ID. Your subscription applies to all businesses you own.

### Do team members need subscriptions?

No, staff members and team members **do not need subscriptions**. Only the business owner needs to subscribe.

### What happens to my data when I downgrade?

**All data is preserved.** Nothing is deleted. Businesses become read-only if they exceed your new tier's limit.

### Can I change which businesses are active after downgrading?

Yes, contact support or use the "Choose Active Businesses" option in settings (if available on your tier).

### What if I delete a business by accident?

Business deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Always verify before deleting.

### Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple according to their refund policy. To request a refund:
1. Open App Store
2. Tap your profile icon
3. Tap **Purchase History**
4. Find Business Manager Pro
5. Tap **Report a Problem**

**Note:** Refunds result in immediate downgrade to Free tier.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open **Settings** app on your device
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Business Manager Pro**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

### Can I upgrade mid-cycle?

Yes, you can upgrade anytime. You'll receive credit for the unused portion of your current subscription.

### Do I need to cancel before upgrading?

No, upgrading automatically cancels your current subscription and starts the new one.

### What payment methods are accepted?

Business Manager Pro uses Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment methods accepted by Apple App Store are supported:
- Credit/debit cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- App Store gift cards
- Carrier billing (if available)

### Can I pay annually?

Yes, annual subscriptions are available at a discount:
- Pro: $99.99/year (save $19.89)
- Pro Plus: $199.99/year (save $39.89)
- Max: $499.99/year (save $99.89)

### What happens if my payment fails?

Apple will attempt to collect payment for several days. If payment continues to fail:
1. You'll receive notifications from Apple
2. Subscription enters "grace period"
3. After grace period, subscription cancels
4. On next open, downgrade flow begins

Update your payment method in App Store settings to avoid interruption.

### Can I use the app offline?

Yes, most features work offline:
- Create sales
- Manage products
- View reports
- Manage customers

Data syncs when you reconnect to internet.

### Is my subscription shared with family?

No, Business Manager Pro subscriptions are **not** part of Apple Family Sharing. Each user needs their own subscription.

### Can I transfer my subscription to another account?

No, subscriptions are tied to your Apple ID and cannot be transferred.

### What happens to my business if I'm added as an owner to another business?

You can only be the owner of businesses you create. Being added to another business makes you a staff member, not an owner.

### I'm on Free tier with 1 business, and I'm staff on 5 others. Do I need to upgrade?

No, staff membership on other businesses is completely free and doesn't count toward your limit.

### Can I split ownership of a business?

No, each business has one owner. However, you can add team members with "Admin" role for nearly full control.

### How do I export my data?

1. Open Reports screen
2. Tap the export button (share icon)
3. Choose format (CSV or PDF)
4. Data includes sales, products, customers, expenses

### Can I import data from another system?

Yes, Business Manager Pro supports importing:
- Products from CSV
- Customers from CSV
- Sales from CSV (with limitations)

Contact support for import templates and guidance.
