# Legal Disclaimer Update Summary

**Date:** November 2025
**Status:** Completed
**Files Modified:** 2

## Overview

Updated the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy to clearly disclaim that all reports, analytics, and financial summaries generated by Business Manager Pro are for basic business understanding and informational purposes only, and are NOT suitable for compliance, tax, legal, or official accounting purposes.

## Why This Was Necessary

Business Manager Pro generates various reports including:
- Sales summaries and revenue reports
- Profit and loss calculations
- Income statements
- Cash flow analyses
- Expense reports by category
- Product performance analytics
- Customer purchase analytics

While these reports are valuable for day-to-day business understanding, they are:
- **Not certified** by accounting professionals
- **Not audited** by external auditors
- **Not compliant** with tax regulations
- **Not suitable** for official submissions to government agencies
- **Not verified** for accuracy by professionals
- **Not aligned** with GAAP, IFRS, or other accounting standards

## Changes Made

### File 1: Terms and Conditions (terms.tsx)

#### New Section Added: "7. Reports and Analytics Disclaimer"

**Key Points Added:**

1. **Informational Purpose Only**
   - All reports and analytics are for basic business understanding only
   - Not intended for any compliance use

2. **Not for Compliance Use**
   - Explicitly lists what reports should NOT be used for:
     - Tax filing or tax compliance
     - Official financial statements for regulatory bodies
     - Audited financial reporting
     - Legal proceedings or court submissions
     - Bank loan applications or credit assessments
     - Government regulatory compliance
     - Certified accounting or bookkeeping requirements

3. **Professional Consultation Required**
   - Users must consult with qualified professionals:
     - Certified Public Accountants (CPAs)
     - Licensed tax professionals
     - Financial auditors
     - Legal advisors
     - Certified bookkeepers

4. **No Guarantee of Accuracy**
   - No warranties or guarantees on report accuracy
   - No promise of completeness or reliability

5. **Calculation Methodology Limitations**
   - Basic mathematical calculations only
   - Does not account for:
     - Complex tax regulations and deductions
     - Depreciation and amortization
     - Accrual accounting principles
     - GAAP or IFRS standards
     - Industry-specific requirements

6. **User Responsibility**
   - Users accept full responsibility for:
     - Verifying all data
     - Consulting appropriate professionals
     - Ensuring compliance with applicable laws

#### Updated Section: "10. Limitation of Liability"

Added two new subsections:

**10.3. Business Decisions:**
- Not liable for decisions based on reports/analytics
- Reports are general information tools only
- Should not be relied upon without professional verification

**10.4. Tax and Compliance Liability:**
- Not responsible for tax liabilities or penalties
- Not liable for legal issues from report use/misuse
- Users solely responsible for compliance

3. **Professional Consultation Requirement**
   - Explicitly requires users to consult professionals
